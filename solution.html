Magnetic materials

Magnetic materials are divided into two categories: permanent magnetic materials and soft magnetic materials, which belong to hard and brittle materials. Diamond is used as abrasive for grinding this material. Jinzuan has developed a special production process for magnetic materials to ensure that there will be no chipping or burrs during cutting, and it has the characteristics of high precision, narrow kerf and high processing efficiency.

Ceramics

Due to the high hardness and high brittleness of ceramic materials, most of the processed ceramic components will produce various types of surface or subsurface damage, which will lead to a decrease in the strength of ceramic components, which in turn limits the adoption of high material removal rates.

The fundamental goal for efficient grinding of ceramics is to achieve maximum material removal rates while maintaining material surface integrity and dimensional accuracy. JINZUAN grinding wheel can maintain high efficiency while also maintaining good surface quality on ceramic surfaces.
