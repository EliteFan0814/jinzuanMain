Vitrified CBN Grinding Wheels

  • Home
  • Products
  • Description
  • Main Features

Vitrified bond diamond grinding wheels have been widely used in the PCD/PCBN tools edge processing, diamond edge processing, carbide cylindrical processing and camshaft grinding and other processing fields. Vitrified bond grinding wheels will produce a lot of heat in the process of high load during dry grinding. Using the cooling fluid with vitrified grinding wheel the effect is more outstanding. The diameter range is 10 ~ 900mm; the thickness range is 3 ~ 300 mm; the grit size range is 80 ~ 5000#. We can also design and make nonstandard grinding wheels according to customers' requirement.

  • 1. Sharp cutting
  • 2. High efficiency
  • 3. Less heat and jam generated in grinding
  • 4. Easy control of the precision
  • 5. Easy dressing
  • 6. Saving time
  • 7. Long life
  • 8. Saving cost
