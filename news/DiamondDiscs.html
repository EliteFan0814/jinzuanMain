Diamond discs(Blades)

  • Home
  • news

Diamond cutting discs, that is, the main body is made of high-strength, high-rigidity steel plate, and the outer part is the diamond working layer. The connection methods between the working layer and the main body mainly include metal bond, resin bond, ceramic bond, etc. In addition, there are electroplating or brazing methods to fix the diamond on the surface of the base metal.

The high-rigidity metal plate is closely connected with the diamond working layer, which can realize high-load, deep cutting, and is suitable for cutting and grooving of various materials. Its main features are:

  • 1. High precision, can be used for precision grooving and cutting, can reduce subsequent processing procedures;
  • 2. The slit is narrow, which can greatly improve the utilization rate of precious raw materials;
  • 3. High efficiency, sharp cutting, no need for frequent tool changes;
  • 4. High rigidity, high strength, and long service life;

Our company's flagship products are bronze sintered diamond cutting discs and resin bonded diamond cutting discs. The abrasive layer of bronze sintered diamond cutting discs has strong bonding force, good wear resistance, good edge shape retention and long service life. The resin bonded cutting discs have the advantages of good self-sharpening, sharp cutting, high cutting efficiency, easy deep cutting and fast feeding, elastic bonding agent, good processing quality and surface finish.

The main features of the diamond cutting discs produced by our company are:

  • 1. Good cutting ability and high wear resistance ensure the blade has a long service life;
  • 2. The high hardness of the bonding agent and the high rigidity of the base metal plate keep the blade from deforming during the cutting process, thus ensuring the verticality of the cutting surface;
  • 3. Through the fine adjustment of the concentration and particle size of the abrasive, good cutting efficiency can be achieved while meeting the requirements for cutting surface smoothness.
  • 4. With the exclusive production technology of ultra-thin cutting discs, we can produce cutting discs with a thickness as thin as 0.3mm and large-scale cutting discs with a diameter of more than 500mm. Through advanced scientific production technology and strict quality control, the finished products are guaranteed to have high precision, whether it is flatness, verticality or thickness deviation;
  • 5. Suitable for single as well as gang wheel applications;
  • 6. The abrasive can be diamond or CBN.
